Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
MOQ varies by supplier and product. You can filter search results by MOQ range. Many suppliers offer sample orders with no minimum.
How does Trade Assurance protect me?
Your payment is held securely until you confirm the goods are delivered as agreed. If there's a dispute, our team mediates and ensures a fair resolution.
Can I negotiate price with suppliers?
Absolutely. You can chat directly with any supplier, request custom quotes, and negotiate pricing, packaging, and lead times in real time.
How do I verify a supplier's authenticity?
Look for the "Verified Pro" or "Gold Supplier" badges. These indicate the supplier has passed our identity checks, on-site audits, and quality assessments.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept bank transfers, major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and Noshahibaba escrow. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
How long does shipping typically take?
Shipping time depends on origin, destination, and method chosen. Express air freight takes 3–7 days; sea freight takes 15–45 days for international orders.
