Our Journey

From Lahore to the World

Noshahibaba was boldly founded in 2020 in the vibrant heart of Lahore, Pakistan, with a singular, powerful mission: to absolutely democratize global trade and make it accessible to resilient businesses of every kind and size.

What initially began as a small, determined digital platform dedicated to aggressively connecting brilliant local textile and raw material manufacturers directly with eager international buyers, has miraculously exploded into one of the largest, most trusted, and continuously innovative B2B marketplaces originating from South Asia.

Our visionary founder, intensely inspired by the staggering raw potential, immense diversity, and intricate craftsmanship of regional manufacturing, clearly observed a painful barrier. Thousands of incredibly talented producers were simply unable to ever reach lucrative global markets purely due to a rigid lack of digital infrastructure, immense linguistic hurdles, and an utter absence of standardized trust mechanisms. Noshahibaba was passionately built from the ground up to aggressively completely obliterate exactly that problem.

Today, we humbly yet proudly serve over 10 million spectacularly active buyers alongside 50,000 strictly verified dedicated suppliers spanning across 190 dynamic countries globally. With well over $5 billion in verified trade seamlessly facilitated directly through our secure platform so far, our ambitious journey is truly only just beginning. We remain relentlessly fiercely committed to our core founding principle: to continually make intricate global trade simple, profoundly safe, radically transparent, and emphatically fair for everyone involved.
