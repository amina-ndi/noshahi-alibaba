Our Core Protections

NoshahiBaba Trade Assurance is designed to give you peace of mind throughout your entire sourcing journey.

Safe Payment

Your payment is held in escrow and only released to the supplier after you confirm satisfactory receipt of your order.

Shipping Guarantee

Get your money back if the supplier fails to ship your order on the agreed date or if the shipping doesn't meet quality standards.

Product Quality

Your order is covered if the product quality doesn't match the specifications in your purchase contract.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Trade Assurance protection.

Is Trade Assurance free to use?

Yes, Trade Assurance is a free service provided by NoshahiBaba.com to help build trust between buyers and suppliers.

What payment methods are supported?

We support Visa, Mastercard, T/T (Bank Transfer), Online Bank Payment, and Western Union.

How do I open a dispute?

You can open a dispute in your Order Management dashboard if you are not satisfied with the quality or delivery timing.
